About Me

I work as a PhD student in the mathematical physics team at Laboratoire d'Annecy-le-Vieux de Physique Théorique in France.

I'm interested in algebraic objects appearing in the field of integrable systems.

More precisely, I study with my director E. Ragoucy and codirector L. Poulain d'Andecy algebras and orthogonal polynomials related to classical integrable systems that are out of equilibrium.
